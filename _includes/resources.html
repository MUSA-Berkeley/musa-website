For Alumni

If you’d like to stay in touch with our undergraduate math community even after graduating, please send an email to musa.berkeley@gmail.com and we will keep you informed of opportunities to contribute in the future. You can contribute by giving Math Monday talks or sharing your experience in professional or academic panels. We promise not to spam you!
