Berkeley Undergraduate Mathementoring Program

BUMP (function), or the Berkeley Undergraduate Mathementoring Program (for undergraduates not capable of taking initiative out of nervousness), is MUSA's mentoring program.

BUMP mentors, who are junior or senior volunteers, meet with their students, first and second-years, weekly or biweekly. MUSA occasionally sponsors special events for BUMP, but mentors and groups of mentors are responsible for holding most of the events.

While signups are closed for Spring 2018, keep an eye on our mailing list if you're interested in the fall!
